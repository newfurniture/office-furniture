The History and Myth of Pottery Barn Bedding Canada

Pottery Barn has been a leader in the home decor industry for over 70 years. The brand was founded in 1949 by Paul Secon and his brother Morris, who started the company by selling their own pottery and home accessories. Over the years, Pottery Barn expanded its product offerings to include furniture, lighting, and of course, bedding. Today, Pottery Barn is known for its timeless designs, exceptional quality, and dedication to customer satisfaction.

Despite its name, Pottery Barn does not actually sell pottery. The founders chose the name because they wanted to evoke a sense of craftsmanship and authenticity, similar to that of a traditional pottery barn. This commitment to quality and craftsmanship is evident in every piece of Pottery Barn bedding, which is meticulously designed and crafted to ensure the highest level of comfort and style.
